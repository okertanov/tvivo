What is it?

If you use Picasa to organize and edit your images, you may consider improving its functionality with Tvivo - a tool for publication of images directly into microblogging services. Thus, using the Tvivo button for Picasa, you can publish your pictures to Twitter with Twitpic or Yfrog directly from Picasa. Based on your feedback, we plan to increase the number of services supported by the Tvivo button for Picasa in the future.

Just imagine that your whole image library is before you in Picasa, and all your best photos can be made available to your followers only with a few clicks.

As usual, you simply choose a photo that in your opinion is worthy of publication, click the Tvivo button, add a short text and Voila! -- the photo goes directly into your post on Twitter.

To install, just click on the Tvivo Button link to go to the illustrated installation manual for the Tvivo Button for Picasa. If you have never used Picasa before, you can check it out by going to Google's website and installing it on your computer absolutely free of charge.

Thank you for your interest in our product! We hope that you will enjoy using our Tvivo Button for Picasa.
