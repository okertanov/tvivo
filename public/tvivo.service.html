Share your photos on the microblogging services with Tvivo

If you have read the home page carefully, you already know what it is. Now we will help you to add a new function to your Picasa by installing the Tvivo Button for Picasa on your computer.

Pisaca's infrastructure known as PWU (Picasa Web Uploader) allows to integrate third-party image uploaders without downloading any executable code. A PWU service button is basically just an icon of the button itself with a text description in XML that Picasa can understand. Therefore, your computer cannot be in danger of virus infection as a result of this installation, unlike the binary plug-ins of other software products.

First of all, make sure that Picasa is installed on your computer and is fully functional. Next, to install the Tvivo Button for Picasa, read the step-by-step instructions below, and then click the large Install button.



Installation of the Tvivo Button for Picasa:


Click Install on this page. Click Yes to confirm installation in the Picasa dialog box that appears.


After Picasa is launched, you will see a Configure Buttons dialog, in which you need to click Add and drag the Tvivo Button from the Available Buttons section into the Current Buttons section. Then click Ok.


This is it, the Tvivo Button is now in its place on the panel and you can start uploading images.


To upload, chose a photo in one of your collections in Picasa and click the Tvivo Button. Add text if you wish, then enter your Twitter name and password, and click Publish -- the photo you chose will go to the birds right away.
